Privacy Policy for BrusselFever

Last updated: May 15, 2026

BrusselFever is a private nightlife community app for Brussels. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the app.

1. Information we collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Email address, used for account access and authentication.
  • Profile information, such as your pseudonym and optional profile picture.
  • Location information, only when you choose to check in or find nearby venues.
  • Presence information, such as the venue where you choose to show your presence and the related timestamp.
  • Activity reports, such as venue atmosphere votes.
  • Technical information needed to operate and secure the app.

BrusselFever does not track your location continuously in the background.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Let you sign in securely with an email access code.
  • Show nightlife events and venues.
  • Allow you to check in at nearby venues.
  • Show your presence to other invited members when you choose to activate it.
  • Improve, debug, and secure the app.
  • Respond to support requests.

3. Location data

BrusselFever uses your location only when you actively request a location-based action, such as checking in at a nearby venue or validating your presence.

The app does not collect continuous background location data.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal data.

Your pseudonym and optional presence may be visible to other invited BrusselFever members when you choose to activate your presence.

We use third-party service providers to operate the app, including hosting, authentication, database, storage, analytics, crash/debugging, and map services. These providers may process data only as needed to provide their services.

5. Data storage and security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

6. Data retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Presence and activity data may expire or become inactive after a limited period, depending on the app’s functionality.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data.

To request account deletion or data access, contact us at: denaesvin@gmail.com

8. Children

BrusselFever is not intended for children under 13. The app is designed for nightlife-related use and is intended for invited users only.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
