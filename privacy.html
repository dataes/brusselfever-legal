Privacy Policy for BrusselFever

Last updated: May 15, 2026

BrusselFever is a private nightlife community app for Brussels. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the app.

1. Information we collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Email address, used for account access and authentication.
  • Profile information, such as your pseudonym and optional profile picture.
  • Location information, only when you choose to check in, find nearby venues, or validate your presence.
  • Presence information, such as the venue where you choose to show your presence and the related timestamp.
  • Activity reports, such as venue atmosphere votes.
  • User-created content, such as venues or events submitted through the app, if this feature is available to your account.
  • Technical information needed to operate, debug, secure, and improve the app.

BrusselFever does not track your location continuously in the background.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Let you sign in securely with an email access code.
  • Provide account and profile features.
  • Show nightlife events and venues.
  • Allow you to check in at nearby venues.
  • Show your presence to other invited members when you choose to activate it.
  • Display venue activity information.
  • Improve, debug, and secure the app.
  • Respond to support, privacy, and deletion requests.

3. Location data

BrusselFever uses your location only when you actively request a location-based action, such as checking in at a nearby venue, finding nearby venues, or validating your presence.

The app does not collect continuous background location data and does not track your location when you are not using location-based features.

When you choose to show your presence, your pseudonym may be shown as present at a venue to other invited members. BrusselFever does not publicly display your raw GPS coordinates.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal data.

Your pseudonym and optional presence may be visible to other invited BrusselFever members when you choose to activate your presence.

We may use third-party service providers to operate the app, including hosting, authentication, database, storage, analytics, crash/debugging, email delivery, and map services. These providers may process data only as needed to provide their services.

5. Data storage and security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Data transmitted between the app and our services is encrypted in transit. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

6. Data retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, secure the service, and respond to support or privacy requests.

Presence and activity data may expire or become inactive after a limited period, depending on the app’s functionality.

7. Account deletion and data deletion

You can request deletion of your BrusselFever account and associated data by contacting us at: denaesvin@gmail.com

Please contact us from the email address linked to your BrusselFever account so we can verify your request.

When your account is deleted, we delete or anonymize your account information, profile information, pseudonym, profile picture, presence records, activity reports, and account-related data.

Some technical logs or legally required records may be retained for a limited period where necessary for security, fraud prevention, debugging, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

You can also request deletion of specific data without deleting your full account, such as your profile picture, presence information, activity reports, or user-created content.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: denaesvin@gmail.com

9. Children

BrusselFever is not intended for children under 13. The app is designed for nightlife-related use and is intended for invited users only.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
